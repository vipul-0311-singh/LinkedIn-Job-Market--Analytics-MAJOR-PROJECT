# 📊 LinkedIn Job Market Intelligence Dashboard

## Buisiness Problem:
The job market is constantly changing, and job seekers often struggle to identify the right career opportunities due to a lack of insights into hiring trends, required skills, salary expectations, and location-based demand.
Companies and professionals need a data-driven approach to understand:
1.Which job roles have the highest demand?
2.Which skills are most valuable in the market?
3.Which companies are actively hiring?
4.How salaries vary by role, experience, and location?
5.Which opportunities best match an individual's skills and preferences?

This project aims to analyze LinkedIn job data and provide actionable career insights through data analytics and a Career Engine that recommends suitable job opportunities based on location, skills, match score, and missing skills.

## 📌 Project Overview

The data job market is growing rapidly, making it difficult for job seekers to identify the most in-demand roles, companies, and locations.

This project analyzes LinkedIn job posting datasets to generate actionable insights on hiring trends, salary distribution, experience levels, work types, and job locations. The project demonstrates the complete Data Analytics workflow from data cleaning to interactive dashboard creation.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze LinkedIn job market trends
- Identify top hiring companies
- Compare salaries across experience levels
- Understand remote vs onsite job distribution
- Visualize hiring locations worldwide
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard
- Career Recommendation Engine

## 📌 Key Insights

- Average salary is approximately **72.31K**
- More than **100K** job postings were analyzed.
- Full-time jobs dominate the market.
- Executive-level roles offer the highest average salaries.
- Remote opportunities are significantly lower than onsite jobs.
- Major technology companies contribute a large share of data hiring.
